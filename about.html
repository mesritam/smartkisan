About SmartKisan

Our mission, our values, and why we exist for Indian farmers.

Our Mission

SmartKisan was founded with one simple belief: every Indian farmer deserves access to the same quality of information that large agri-businesses have.

Farming is the backbone of India. Yet millions of farmers still make decisions based on guesswork — unaware of accurate weather forecasts, best market prices, or the right time to sow or harvest. We are here to change that.

Through technology, local language support, and a dedicated team of agricultural experts, SmartKisan bridges the information gap between farmers and modern farming knowledge.

Our Vision

We envision an India where no farmer is left behind — where technology works as hard as the farmer does, in the fields and in the markets.

By 2030, we aim to reach 1 million farmers across all 29 states, providing free, reliable, and actionable farming guidance in every major Indian language.

We believe that a better-informed farmer means better harvests, better incomes, and a more prosperous rural India.
